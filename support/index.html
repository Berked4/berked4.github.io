Frequently asked questions

Is my progress stored online?

No. Bouncy Pop does not currently use cloud saving. Your progress is stored locally on your device. When Google Play Games is available, the game may keep separate local progress slots for different Play Games profiles, but the progress itself is not uploaded to a Diri Games server.

What happens if I uninstall the game?

Local progress may be deleted and may not be recoverable. Bouncy Pop does not currently use a Diri Games cloud-save service.

How do I manage ad privacy choices?

Where available or required, use the Privacy Options in the game's Settings. You can also manage your device's advertising ID in Android system settings.

Are purchases active?

Yes. Bouncy Pop includes an optional one-time Premium Pack purchase processed through Google Play Billing. Premium ownership can be restored through the game when Google Play confirms ownership.

Are online leaderboards active?

Yes. Bouncy Pop uses Google Play Games Services for its global Total Score leaderboard. Leaderboard availability depends on Google Play Games sign-in, network access, and Google's services.

Does Bouncy Pop create an account?

No. Bouncy Pop does not create a Diri Games account. Google Play Games is an optional Google service used for leaderboards and for selecting separate local progress slots.
