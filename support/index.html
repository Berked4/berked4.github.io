Frequently asked questions

Is my progress stored online?

No. Your progress is stored locally on your device only.

What happens if I uninstall the game?

Local progress may be deleted and cannot be recovered, because there is no account system or server backup.

How do I manage ad privacy choices?

Where available or required, use the Privacy Options in the game's Settings. You can also manage your device's advertising ID in Android system settings.

Are purchases active?

Real-money purchases are not active yet.

Are online leaderboards active?

Not yet.
