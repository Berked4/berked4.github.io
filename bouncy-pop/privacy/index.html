Privacy Policy — Bouncy Pop

Bouncy Pop is a casual arcade game developed and operated by Diri Games (Berke Diri).

Effective date: July 9, 2026
Contact: contact.dirigames@gmail.com

Summary

Bouncy Pop is designed to work offline. You do not need an account to play, and Diri Games does not operate any servers that collect your personal data. Your game progress is stored only on your own device.

Advertising in Bouncy Pop is provided by Google AdMob, a service of Google LLC. As a third-party advertising provider, Google may process certain device data to serve and measure ads. This is explained below.

Information stored on your device

Bouncy Pop saves your game data locally on your device only. This includes:

  • Coins
  • Total Score
  • Level progress
  • Skins and unlocks, and your selected skin
  • Settings such as language, sound, and haptics

This data stays on your device. It is not sent to Diri Games, and we cannot see it.

Please note: if you uninstall the app or clear the app's data, your progress and settings may be permanently deleted. Because there is no account system and no server backup, we cannot recover lost progress.

No account and no backend

  • Bouncy Pop has no registration, login, or social sign-in.
  • Diri Games does not operate a server backend for the game.
  • We do not create or store any player profile, and we do not collect your name, email address, or other personal information through the game.

Advertising

Bouncy Pop shows ads provided by Google AdMob:

  • Optional rewarded ads. You may choose to watch an ad to receive extra in-game rewards, such as 2× coins, 2× score, or a Bonus Pod 2× bonus. Watching these ads is always your choice.
  • Interstitial ads. Short full-screen ads may appear between levels.

To serve, limit, measure, and protect ads, Google AdMob may collect and process data from your device, such as:

  • your device's Advertising ID
  • your IP address
  • general device and app information
  • ad interaction data (for example, which ads were shown or tapped)

Diri Games does not receive or store this data. It is handled by Google under Google's own policies. For more information, see:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services

Ad privacy choices and consent

  • Consent message. Where required by law (for example, in the European Economic Area and the United Kingdom), a consent message may be shown before ads are displayed. Your choice is stored and respected.
  • Personalized or non-personalized ads. Where available, you may choose between personalized and non-personalized ads. Non-personalized ads may still use limited data for basic ad delivery, frequency capping (limiting how often you see the same ad), and fraud prevention.
  • Changing your choice. Where available or required, you may be able to review and change your ad privacy choices from the in-game Settings.
  • Android controls. Android also lets you manage your device's advertising ID (for example, resetting or deleting it) in your device's system settings.

Purchases

Bouncy Pop currently does not offer real-money purchases.

If real-money purchases (such as a Premium Pack) are added in a future update, they will be processed by Google Play, and this policy will be updated before or when that feature becomes available. Diri Games would not receive or store your payment card details.

Leaderboards and Google Play Games

An online leaderboard through Google Play Games is not currently active in Bouncy Pop.

If online leaderboards are added in a future update, this policy will be updated to describe how Google Play Games Services is used before or when that feature becomes available.

Children

Bouncy Pop is not directed at children. Diri Games does not collect personal information from anyone through the game, and we do not knowingly allow the collection of personal information from children. Advertising-related data is handled by Google as described in the Advertising section above.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that a child has provided personal data through third-party services used by the game (such as Google's advertising services), please contact us at contact.dirigames@gmail.com and we will help address the concern.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time, for example when new features (such as purchases or online leaderboards) are added. Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date. Meaningful changes will be reflected clearly in the text.
