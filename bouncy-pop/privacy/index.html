Privacy Policy — Bouncy Pop

Bouncy Pop is a casual arcade game developed and operated by Diri Games (Berke Diri).

Effective date: July 23, 2026
Contact: contact.dirigames@gmail.com

Summary

Bouncy Pop can be played without creating a Diri Games account. Diri Games does not operate a game backend, account database, or cloud-save service.

Most game progress and settings are stored locally on the player's device.

Bouncy Pop may use the following Google services:

  • Google Play Games Services for sign-in, player identity, leaderboards, and profile-specific local progress selection.
  • Google Play Billing for the Premium Pack purchase and ownership restoration.
  • Google AdMob for optional rewarded ads and interstitial ads.
  • Google User Messaging Platform for advertising consent and privacy choices where required.

These Google services may process information according to Google's own privacy policies.

Information stored locally on your device

Bouncy Pop stores gameplay and settings data locally on your device. This may include:

  • Coins.
  • Total Score and per-level scores.
  • Current and highest unlocked level.
  • Skins, effects, upgrades, unlocks, and equipped selections.
  • Reward and advertisement claim records used to prevent duplicate rewards.
  • Advertisement cooldown and eligibility information.
  • Language, sound, and haptic settings.
  • Premium ownership cache and entitlement status.
  • The last confirmed Play Games progress profile.
  • Separate local progress snapshots associated with different Play Games profiles.

This data is used to save progress, restore settings, prevent duplicate rewards, and maintain separate local progress for different Play Games profiles.

Except for leaderboard scores and information processed through the Google services described below, Diri Games does not receive this locally stored gameplay data because Diri Games does not operate a game server.

If you uninstall Bouncy Pop, clear the app's storage, or reset the device, some or all local progress and settings may be permanently deleted. Diri Games cannot recover locally deleted progress.

Google Play Games Services

Bouncy Pop may connect to Google Play Games Services.

Depending on your Google Play Games settings and authorization status, the app may receive information made available by Google, such as:

  • Your Google Play Games player identifier.
  • Your Play Games display name or other public profile information.
  • Your leaderboard score and rank.
  • Leaderboard entries belonging to other players.

Bouncy Pop uses your Play Games player identifier inside the native Android application to create a hashed, pseudonymous profile key. This key is stored locally and is used to keep separate local progress slots for different Play Games profiles.

The raw Play Games player identifier is not sent to a Diri Games server. Diri Games does not operate a server that stores Play Games identifiers or profile hashes.

Changing Play Games accounts may cause the game to load a different locally stored progress slot. Returning to a previously used Play Games account may restore that account's previous local progress on the same device.

This is not cloud saving. Gameplay progress is not uploaded to a Diri Games server and may not transfer automatically to another device.

Leaderboards

Bouncy Pop may submit your persistent Total Score to a Google Play Games leaderboard.

Google Play Games may process and display:

  • Your submitted Total Score.
  • Your leaderboard rank.
  • Your Play Games display name and public profile information.
  • Scores and rankings of other players.

The app may display Top Players and ranking entries near the current player.

Leaderboard data is processed by Google Play Games Services under Google's own terms and privacy policies. Diri Games does not operate a separate leaderboard server.

You can manage your Google Play Games profile and privacy controls through your Google account and Google Play Games settings.

Purchases and Premium Pack

Bouncy Pop offers an optional one-time in-app purchase called the Premium Pack.

Purchases are processed through Google Play Billing.

Google Play may provide the app with information required to complete, acknowledge, and restore a purchase, such as:

  • Product identifier.
  • Localized product title and price.
  • Purchase status.
  • Ownership status.
  • Google-issued purchase identifiers or tokens.

Diri Games does not receive or store your payment card number, bank account information, or other payment credentials. Payment processing is handled by Google Play.

The app may process purchase status locally to unlock and restore Premium features.

Premium entitlement is verified through Google Play Billing and is independent from the Play Games profile used for local gameplay progress.

Premium removes forced interstitial advertising. Optional rewarded advertising may remain available to Premium players.

Advertising

Bouncy Pop may display advertising provided by Google AdMob.

Advertising formats may include:

  • Optional rewarded ads that players may choose to watch in exchange for an additional in-game reward.
  • Interstitial ads that may appear between levels for non-Premium players.

Rewarded advertising is optional. If an advertisement is unavailable, fails to load, is closed without earning a reward, or cannot be displayed, the related reward is not granted.

To provide, limit, measure, secure, and personalize advertising where permitted, Google AdMob may process information such as:

  • Advertising ID or similar device identifiers.
  • IP address.
  • General device, operating system, and application information.
  • Advertising interactions, impressions, and clicks.
  • Diagnostic, fraud-prevention, and ad-performance information.
  • Consent and privacy-choice information.

Diri Games does not operate an advertising-data server and does not directly store this information. Google processes this data under its own policies.

For more information, see:

  • Google Privacy Policy:
    https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services:
    https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

Ad privacy choices and consent

Where required by applicable law, Bouncy Pop may display a privacy or consent message using Google's User Messaging Platform before requesting advertising.

Depending on availability and legal requirements, users may be able to:

  • Consent to or reject certain advertising purposes.
  • Choose personalized or non-personalized advertising where those choices are offered.
  • Review or change advertising privacy choices from the in-game Settings screen.
  • Manage or reset the Android Advertising ID through Android system settings.

Non-personalized or limited advertising may still use certain information for basic delivery, frequency control, security, fraud prevention, and measurement.

Support communications

Bouncy Pop does not collect your email address through normal gameplay.

If you voluntarily contact Diri Games by email, we may receive:

  • Your email address.
  • Your name, if included in your email account or message.
  • The contents of your support request.
  • Attachments or diagnostic information you choose to send.

This information is used only to respond to the request, investigate a reported issue, maintain necessary support records, and protect against abuse.

You may request deletion of support correspondence by contacting contact.dirigames@gmail.com, subject to any information that must be retained for legitimate legal, security, or dispute-resolution purposes.

Accounts, data deletion, and retention

Bouncy Pop does not create a separate Diri Games account.

Because there is no Diri Games account system, there is no Diri Games account-deletion function.

You can remove locally stored Bouncy Pop data by:

  • Clearing the app's storage in Android settings.
  • Uninstalling the app.

These actions may permanently delete local progress and settings.

Information managed by Google Play Games, Google Play Billing, Google AdMob, or your Google account is controlled by Google and may be managed through the relevant Google account, Google Play, Play Games, advertising, or privacy settings.

Diri Games cannot directly delete or recover data stored only by Google or stored only on your device.

Third-party services

Bouncy Pop currently integrates with services provided by Google LLC, including:

  • Google Play Games Services.
  • Google Play Billing.
  • Google AdMob.
  • Google User Messaging Platform.

The availability and behavior of these services may depend on your device, region, internet connection, Google account, consent choices, and Google's service configuration.

Diri Games does not currently use a separate third-party analytics or crash-reporting service in Bouncy Pop.

Children's privacy

Bouncy Pop is not designed for children under the age of 13 and is not intended to be a child-directed service.

Diri Games does not knowingly create accounts for or directly collect personal information from children through the game.

Advertising, Play Games, and purchase-related information is processed by Google as described in this policy.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that personal information has been provided through Bouncy Pop or a connected third-party service, contact contact.dirigames@gmail.com.

Security

Bouncy Pop uses platform-provided Google Play services and local application storage for the features described in this policy.

No method of electronic processing or storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure. Diri Games takes reasonable steps to limit the information processed directly and does not operate a server containing Bouncy Pop player progress or payment information.

This Privacy Policy may be updated when Bouncy Pop's features, third-party services, legal requirements, or data practices change.

Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
